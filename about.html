Our Story

Mouse's Enchanted Adventures is a magical journey following Mouse, a brave and adventurous tabby cat who embarks on thrilling quests in an enchanted world. Created by Katherine McNeil, this series brings imagination to life through captivating storytelling and vibrant illustrations.

Meet Mouse

Mouse is no ordinary cat—he’s a vampire cat with a heart full of curiosity and courage. Dressed in his iconic red and black cape, he ventures into mystical lands, meeting friends and foes along the way. Join him on his spellbinding adventures!
